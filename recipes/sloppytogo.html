Sloppy Steaks To-Go

Take it To-Go!

In case the watiers at Truffoni's are really hounding you, or you want to enjoy a nice sloppy steak at work, this recipe is for you!

Ingredients

  • An order of Truffoni's steak or leftover home-cooked steak (pre-slopped up)
  • a 12oz bottle of Essnetia Ionized Water

Directions

  1. Place the steak on a microwavable safe palte and heat for 1-2 minutes
  2. Pour Essentia Ionized Water into a glass
  3. Shout "Let's slop em up" as you dump that water on the steak.
  4. Enjoy!
